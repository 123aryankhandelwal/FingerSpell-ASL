FingerSpell-ASL




About 5% population in world are suffering from hearing loss. Deaf and dumb people
use sign language as their primary means to express their thoughts and ideas to the
people around them with different hand and body gestures. We present our trained
recognizer to reduce the communication gap between the physically challenged and
the normal people.It enables the physically challenged to communicate easily with
normal people using American sign language and helps normal people to recognize
different gestures based signs.
